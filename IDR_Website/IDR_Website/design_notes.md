# Brand & Design Choices

## Color Palette
The primary palette utilizes **Orange (#FF5722)**, **Black (#1A1A1A)**, and **White (#FFFFFF)**.
- **Orange** conveys energy, action, and urgency, which aligns with identifying and mitigating risk.
- **Black and White** provide a clean, structural, and professional contrast, cementing the "tech and education" aesthetic.

## Typography
For the logo and headings, a geometric sans-serif font (**Outfit**) and for body copy (**Inter**) were chosen to suggest modernity, clarity, and precision. It creates a highly legible text block that works both at tiny favicon sizes and on large billboard applications.

## Symbolism
The logo revolves around a 3D isometric **cube**. A cube fundamentally represents structure, stability, and building blocks—essential concepts for an institute focusing on education and establishing resilient risk frameworks. The sharp angles reflect the high-consequence environments IDR deals with, and its segmented faces suggest the multi-faceted nature of digital and AI risk.
